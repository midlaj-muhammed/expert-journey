Data Scientist

Company: TechInnovate Inc.
Location: Remote (US-based)

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Data Scientist to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in machine learning, statistical analysis, and data visualization. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to extract insights from complex datasets and develop predictive models to drive business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement machine learning models to solve complex business problems
- Perform statistical analysis on large datasets to identify patterns and trends
- Create data visualizations and dashboards to communicate findings to stakeholders
- Collaborate with engineering teams to deploy models to production
- Stay current with the latest developments in machine learning and data science
- Mentor junior data scientists and analysts

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field (Master's preferred)
- 3+ years of experience in data science or related field
- Proficiency in Python and its data science libraries (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn)
- Experience with SQL and database systems
- Knowledge of machine learning algorithms and statistical modeling
- Familiarity with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop)
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Experience with NLP or computer vision projects
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Published research papers in relevant fields

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and equity options
- Flexible work schedule and remote work options
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) matching
- Professional development budget
- Unlimited PTO policy
